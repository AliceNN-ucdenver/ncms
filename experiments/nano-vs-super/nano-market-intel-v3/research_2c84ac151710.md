## Jobs‑to‑Be‑Done Analysis  
- **Primary job:** Users need to **securely access market‑research data and analytical tools across multiple platforms while maintaining compliance and minimal friction**. (Evidence: community demand for seamless SSO and adaptive authentication – **S15**, **S14**.)  
- **Underserved outcomes:** Current identity solutions **lack adaptive, AI‑driven risk assessment and parser‑resistant SSO**; community discussions (e.g., “Sign in as anyone: Bypassing SAML SSO authentication with parser differentials” – **HN discussion 1**) highlight successful bypasses, indicating gaps.  
- **Overserved outcomes:** Some platforms over‑engineer with extensive legacy federation and customization that exceed the needs of small research teams, leading to unnecessary complexity (evidence: “Ask HN: SSO services, Auth0 vs Stormpath vs DailyCred” – **HN discussion 5**).
